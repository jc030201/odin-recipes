Gotcha Roast Pork

An innovative dish using potatoes and seasoning to imitate meat.

Ingredients

  • White Potatoes
  • Eringi Mushrooms
  • White Onions
  • Rosemary
  • Butter
  • Thick-cut Bacon
  • Red Wine
  • Sake
  • Soy Sauce
  • Parsley (or any other garnish)

Recipe

  1. Peel, slice, and then steam the potatoes.
  2. Chop the mushrooms and onions into equally-sized cubes.
  3. Pan fry the onions and mushrooms.
  4. Mash the potatoes, then combine the onion and mushroom mix into the mash.
  5. Add the salt to the bottom of the bowl.
  6. Form the fake roast, wrap with bacon, and tie the roast.
  7. Intertwine the rosemary to the roast and then bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes at 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  8. Reduce the wine in the frying pan.
  9. Add the butter, sake, and soy sauce to the wine reduction.
  10. Remove the twine and rosemary from the roast and pour the sauce on the roast
  11. Add garnish and serve.
